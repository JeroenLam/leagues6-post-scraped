Overview
Yama's Lair

The way you begin this league will be unlike anything else you've seen so far as Yama's created a unique version of his domain where you will spawn upon your arrival and as you go through the leagues Tutorial. After that you'll have free reign to explore this new lair which features lots of demons running around making sure everything's in check for the league to commence!

One very nice thing Yama has done for you this league is allowed you to use the stepping stones in the lava pool to train your Agility from level 1. Be careful though as this agility course is slightly different and you're likely to get burnt if you mess up. You'll also be able to come back to the lair at any time if you use your home teleport in the spellbook.

Demonic Pacts

It's time for you to sign over more than just your soul this league as we've taken the previous Combat Masteries system and cranked it up to 666!

Our new system gives you way more flexibility with what combat styles you want to specialise in as the Demonic Pacts form a skill tree, something we're trying for the very first time!

Completing certain combat-related tasks will bag you Pact Points which you can spend at your leisure on any available pacts (any pacts connected to pacts previously unlocked). You'll have a total of 40 points to use, and we have a handy new filter to show you exactly what tasks will gift you with these points!

BE WARNED: while you will have three full tree resets, that's your lot! Take down your first three unique Echo Bosses and earn yourselves those sweet, sweet resets.

We've got a full newspost giving you more information about the system and find out what to expect from the brand new Demonic Pacts! As a bonus, our friends over at the OSRS Wiki have made a wonderful Demonic Pacts Calculator, so you can see exactly what you can unlock and give you a chance to properly experiment before April 15th!

Echo Bosses & Echo Rewards

Echo Bosses are back and they want their revenge! Taking out contracts with Yama has infused them in a devilish new look and they are eager to see you take them on. Of course, you'll need to fight the original version of the bosses to acquire their respective Echo Orbs to unlock this demonic variant.

While the bosses this time around are mostly the same as before, we've reworked the rewards to really lean into the Demonic Pacts and work with different elements of the system!

Point Cap Advice

To help those aiming to earn the most amount of points this league, we thought we'd shed some light on some key information you should know!

For starters, all regions will give the same amount of points to allow for a bit more flexibilty and let you decide on the content you enjoy most while you blaze through your task list.

Secondly, there are a few caveats you'll want to keep in mind when planning which regions you do choose to get the maximum amount of points:

  • You will want at least one raid to get all 3 mega rares
  • You will need to be able to fill 750 Collection Log slots
  • You will need to be able to complete 10 different bosses Combat Achievements
  • You will need to be able to complete 30 CA Speed Tasks
  • You will need to be able to complete 250 CA tasks total
  • You will need to reach the Elite tier of the Combat Achievements
  • Additionally, you'll need to be able to get to 50M XP in each skill

As long as your regions meet the above criteria you should be able to reach the point cap. Any league or diary tasks which an area has which are deemed incompleteable through an oversight will be autocompleted at some point after launch to keep a level of fairness across those competing.

Finally, just to give you a little extra, there's 4 new XP tasks which you might want to keep in mind when choosing your relics:

  • Obtain 100 million experience in a skill excluding Attack, Strength, Defence, Magic, Ranged & Hitpoints
  • Obtain 100 million experience in either Attack, Strength, Defence, Magic, Ranged, Hitpoints or Prayer
  • Obtain 200 million experience in any non-combat skill
  • Obtain 200 million experience in either Attack, Strength, Defence, Magic, Ranged, Hitpoints or Prayer

Best of luck to you all!
