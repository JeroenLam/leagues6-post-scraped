Get Ready For Leagues VI: Demonic Pacts - April 15th

23 March 2026
Overview Yama's Lair Starting Area: Varlamore Area Information Relics ????? Echo Bosses Reveals & Teasers FAQ

It's officially leagues Reveal Season - let's get demonic!

To make the wait a little more bearable before Leagues VI: Demonic Pacts launches on April 15th, we'll be dropping a surprise reveal about the league every day until just before launch and gathering them all here in one handy newspost!

Overview
Rules

Your character starts in Yama's Lair with an exit to Civitas illa Fortis.

Your character begins restricted to the area of Varlamore. As you complete tasks, you can unlock more areas. Karamja will be locked at the start of this league but will be a forced unlocked once you reach your first area unlock. This will be followed by three additional regions you choose yourself. Misthalin is completely inaccessible during this league.

Full information about each of the areas can be found in the Area Selection menu. This can be accessed from the leagues menu in the Journal panel.

All experience gained is heavily increased, and this will further increase as you unlock more relics. Exact multipliers can be viewed in the Relics menu.

Sailing will not be enabled this league and content released during Leagues VI will be reviewed and enabled for the league on a case-by-case basis.

We're not ready to reveal this just yet, you shouldn't be looking here... naughty, naughty!

Each area has an Echo Boss; these are enhanced bosses that pose a higher challenge and drop new unique rewards. Killing the regular boss may drop an Echo orb that lets you access the Echo variant. More information can be found in the area interfaces.

Leagues VI: Demonic Pacts will run from April 15th until the game update on June 10th (a total of 56 days).

Initial Setup

Your character is an Ironman, meaning you cannot trade with other players or use the Grand Exchange.

You will start off in a new area this League, Varlamore.

You will auto complete the following quests:

  • Dragon Slayer I
  • Learning the Ropes
  • The Restless Ghost
  • Rune Mysteries
  • Children of the Sun
  • Desert Treasure II - The Fallen Empire
  • Druidic Ritual
  • Eagles' Peak
  • Elemental Workshop I
  • Fairytale I - Growing Pains
  • Fairytale II - Cure a Queen
  • Lost City
  • Nature Spirit
  • Perilous Moons
  • Priest in Peril
  • Twilight's Promise

You will auto complete the following Combat Achievements:

  • Back to Our Roots
  • Demonic Rebound

You will start with the following stat increases:

  • 3 Herblore
  • 5 Runecraft

A Dramen staff will be added to your Inventory along with the usual starter items so that you can access Fairy rings. If lost, you can reclaim it from the leagues tutor in Yama's lair.

You can also claim a free spade, Impling jar and two Strange devices from the leagues Tutor in Yama's Lair.

As you progress through relic tiers, you'll also gain powerful passive effects starting with:

  • All experience is multiplied by 5x.
  • Items from eligible sources will be 2x as common.
  • Farming ticks will occur every minute instead of every five minutes.
  • Minigame points received are boosted by 4x.
  • Run energy is never drained whilst running.
  • All Clue scrolls will drop as stackable scroll boxes, and clue-step progress is saved between clues.
Content Changes

Bosses & Monsters:

  • Echo Boss rewards are a guaranteed drop over a certain threshold
  • Chambers of Xeric: Challenge Mode, Theatre of Blood: Hard Mode & Tombs of Amascut: 500+ have significantly buffed drop rates and difficulty, ToA and ToB will now provide uniques to all players in the raid if the raid obtains one
  • Wilderness Bosses can be accessed without the Wilderness Medium Diary
  • Zulrah has no damage cap or melee damage immunity
  • Impling spawn rates have increased and you'll be more likely to see rare implings
  • Dragon Implings drop reduced Dragon bones

Items:

  • Most shop stock will not deplete.
  • All Soulreaper axe pieces will drop from every Desert Treasure 2 boss
  • Items that drop as components that require multiple regions to create a usable item will drop as the finished item
  • The Twisted bow, Scythe of vitur, and Tumeken's shadow are available from all three raids
  • Scythe of vitur can be charged with just Blood runes
  • Nihil shards drop in increased quantity from Nex
  • The Leagues Tutor in Yama's Lair can sell Max capes once you are eligible

Access:

  • The Abyss and Zanaris are now counted as general regions, meaning you can access them from anywhere via a fairy ring (This includes the cosmic altar)
  • Slayer Masters will assign you the same tasks, taking into account the areas you have currently unlocked
  • Players cannot enter the Ancient Guthixian Temple from the Chasm of Tears. Why do you think Yama wanted Misthalin gone?
  • Teleports and transportation methods to locations outside of areas you have unlocked are restricted
  • When purchasing your Player Owned House it will be accessible from Aldarin. Your house can only be moved to areas you have unlocked
  • Unlocking new areas will complete various quests and Achievement Diary tasks but will not award any quest completion XP. However, you may complete some quests on your own to gain their XP before unlocking the area which auto-completes it

Misc:

  • Forestry Events are three times as likely to spawn
  • Pets and any pet transmog items that roll over between leagues do not count towards the collection log tasks
  • Pet transmogs will now roll over between leagues
Yama's Lair

The way you begin this league will be unlike anything else you've seen so far as Yama's created a unique version of his domain where you will spawn upon your arrival and as you go through the leagues Tutorial. After that you'll have free reign to explore this new lair which features lots of demons running around making sure everything's in check for the league to commence!

One very nice thing Yama has done for you this league is allowed you to use the stepping stones in the lava pool to train your Agility from level 1. Be careful though as this agility course is slightly different and you're likely to get burnt if you mess up. You'll also be able to come back to the lair at any time if you use your home teleport in the spellbook.

New Starting Area: Varlamore

You may have seen some cryptic messages before that hinted towards this and while we still aren't sure how that happened, Yama is finally ready to confirm your very first area will be Varlamore!

While the Master of Pacts had some bane-related issues with Misthalin, he already wanted to mix things up this league to give everyone the same fresh start and what better place than an area we recently released. A land of wonder, nostalgia and exploration that offers you plenty of what you already seek.

We'll admit, this new region doesn't have everything, but that's half the fun of leagues, discovering new ways to progress and challenge yourself to unfamiliar paths.
